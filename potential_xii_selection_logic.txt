POTENTIAL XII SELECTION — FULL DECISION PROCESS
================================================
Documents every step of selectPotentialLineup() in app/game/auction/page.tsx.
Steps run IN ORDER. Each step can only pick from what's left after the previous step.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
PRE-PROCESSING: VIRTUAL ROLE REMAPPING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Before any selection begins, every All-Rounder in the squad gets a temporary
"virtual role" for this algorithm only. Their real role is restored at the end.

  Bowl rating >= Bat rating + 10, spin style  -->  Spin Bowler
  Bowl rating >= Bat rating + 10, pace style  -->  Pace Bowler
  Bat rating  >= Bowl rating + 10             -->  Batsman
  Within 10 of each other                    -->  Stays as All-Rounder

This affects which bowler caps apply to them during selection.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
GLOBAL CAPS (SET ONCE, CHECKED EVERY STEP)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

These limits are evaluated before any player is added:

  - Overseas cap:              Max 4 overseas players in the XII at any time.
  - Pacer cap:                 Max 5 pace bowlers (virtual role).
  - Out-and-out bowler cap     (Pace Bowler + Spin Bowler combined):
      If current selection has 4+ All-Rounders  -->  max 3 out-and-out bowlers
      Else if squad has an AR with bowl >= 80,
        OR 2+ ARs with bowl >= 75               -->  max 4
      Otherwise                                 -->  max 5

NOTE: The out-and-out cap is DYNAMIC — it's recalculated against the current
working selection each time a player is about to be added.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
STEP 1: SELECT 2 OPENERS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1a. Special Pairs (hard-coded forced pairs)
-------------------------------------------
The following pairs are always opened together if BOTH are in the squad:

  - Virat Kohli + Phil Salt
  - Sunil Narine + Finn Allen
  - Yashasvi Jaiswal + Vaibhav Suryavanshi
  - Travis Head + Abhishek Sharma
  - Shubman Gill + Sai Sudharsan

If a pair is found, both are locked in as openers (subject to OS/bowler caps —
if the pair itself violates caps it is skipped).

1b. Remaining Opener Slots
---------------------------
If a special pair didn't fill both spots, the pool of players flagged
isOpener: true is sorted by rating descending (max(bat, bowl)). Each is added
in turn until 2 openers are chosen, checking caps for each.

1c. Fallback (still fewer than 2 openers)
------------------------------------------
If there still aren't 2 openers (e.g. squad has very few flagged openers), the
highest-rated non-opener Batsman / WK-Batsman / All-Rounder is used as a fill-in.

1d. Cleanup
-----------
  - Openers are added to selected, removed from remaining.
  - Any player flagged onlyOpensOrBenched: true who was NOT chosen as an opener
    is permanently removed from the remaining pool (won't be considered again).


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
STEP 2: WICKETKEEPER
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Checks if a keeper (isWicketkeeper, isPartTimeWk, or role WK-Batsman) was
already picked as one of the openers.

  - If yes: Skip this step entirely.
  - If no:  Sort all remaining keepers by rating descending. Pick the first one
            that passes the OS/bowler caps. If every keeper violates a cap,
            force-pick the highest-rated keeper anyway (caps are overridden).


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
STEP 3a: SPINNER GUARANTEE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Checks if at least one spinner (Spin Bowler role, or spin bowling style) is
already in selected.

  - If yes: Skip.
  - If no (and the squad has at least one spinner): Sort remaining spinners by
    bowling rating descending. Pick the first that passes caps. Only adds ONE
    spinner here.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
STEP 3b: OUT-AND-OUT BOWLER MINIMUM
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Target: at least 3 out-and-out bowlers (Pace Bowler + Spin Bowler combined) in
selected, capped by however many out-and-out bowlers exist in the entire squad.

Sorts the remaining out-and-out bowlers by overall rating descending and adds
them until the minimum is reached, checking caps each time. Stops at 12 total.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
STEP 3c: BOWLING RATING MINIMUMS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Two sub-passes run back to back:

  Pass 1 — Bowl rating > 75 (target: 4 players)
    All remaining players with currentBowling > 75, sorted by bowling rating
    descending. Added one by one until 4 players in selected have bowling > 75.
    Checks caps each time. Stops at 12 players total.

  Pass 2 — Bowl rating > 70 (target: 5 players)
    Same as above but threshold is > 70 and target is 5 players total.
    Sorted by bowling rating descending, added until target hit or 12 reached.

NOTE: These passes count ALL players already in selected (including openers and
the keeper) who meet the threshold — they don't exclusively add bowlers.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
STEP 4: GREEDY FILL TO 12
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

All remaining players not yet in selected are sorted by overall rating
(max(bat, bowl)) descending. Added greedily until selected hits 12, checking
OS and bowler caps for each.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
STEP 5: FORCE-FILL TO 12 (SAFETY NET)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

If selected still has fewer than 12 players AND the squad has 12+ players
(meaning caps blocked someone), the caps are IGNORED and the highest-rated
remaining players are force-added to reach 12.
Players with onlyOpensOrBenched are still excluded.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
STEP 6: SMART OVERSEAS / SUB-75 UPGRADE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Only runs if selected has exactly 12 players.

For each player in selected with rating < 75 (sorted lowest-first):

  1. Find the highest-rated OVERSEAS bench player with a higher rating.
  2. If found and the XII has fewer than 4 OS — directly swap the weak player
     out for the overseas bench player (if caps allow).
  3. If already at 4 OS, try to find a domestic player in the XII to swap out
     for one of the current OS players, freeing a slot for the stronger
     overseas bench player. The domestic swap candidate must be:
       - Same role as the OS player being swapped out
       - Within 3 rating points of the OS player being replaced
       - The resulting XII must still pass all caps

Tiebreaker order when multiple swaps are valid:
  1. Smallest rating difference (closest match)
  2. Swap out the lower-rated OS player first
  3. Swap out the lower-priced OS player first
  4. Swap out the lower-reputation OS player first
  5. Random


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
STEP 7: TRIM TO 11 — LINEUP ORDERING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

The 12-player selection is now ordered into a batting lineup.

7a. Opening Pair Positioning
-----------------------------
Same five special pairs are checked again. If a pair exists in the XII, they
are locked to positions 1 & 2 in that order.

If no special pair, players flagged isOpener: true are sorted by rating
descending and placed at positions 1 & 2. If fewer than 2 flagged openers,
the highest-rated non-opener batter/AR fills in.

7b. Phase 1 — Core Batters (positions 3 onward, up to position 7)
------------------------------------------------------------------
Players from the remaining pool who are:
  - isCoreBatter: true
  - isFinisher: false
  - Role is Batsman, WK-Batsman, or All-Rounder

...are assigned to middle-order slots using the HYBRID SEQUENTIAL algorithm:

For each slot (3 -> 4 -> 5 -> 6 -> 7 in order):
  1. Find all remaining players who PREFER this position
     (hasBattedAt3/4/5/6/7), sorted by bat rating descending.
  2. For each candidate, do a LOOK-AHEAD check: would assigning them leave
     any other player with zero preferred slots remaining? If yes, skip them
     (unless rating > 80, in which case the look-ahead is skipped).
  3. If no candidate passes the look-ahead, fall back to the highest-rated
     player who prefers this slot.
  4. If nobody prefers this slot at all, fall back to the highest-rated
     remaining player overall.

7c. Phase 2 — Finishers (positions after core batters, up to position 7)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
Players flagged isFinisher: true are assigned to the next available slots
using the same hybrid sequential algorithm.

7d. Phase 3 — Remaining Players (position 8 onward)
----------------------------------------------------
All unassigned players with batting rating > 0 are placed next, sorted by
bat rating descending. Pure bowlers (bat = 0) fill the tail, sorted by bowl
rating ascending (weakest bat last).


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
STEP 8: FINISHER PROMOTION TO POSITION 3 OR 4 (MAX ONCE)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Checks positions 3 and 4 (indices 2 and 3) in the draft lineup:

  Position 3 eligible?
    The player there has currentBatting < 78, AND a finisher exists in
    positions 4-11 with isFinisher: true, role Batsman, hasBattedAt3: true,
    and a higher bat rating. Pick the best such candidate.

  Position 4 eligible?
    Same check but hasBattedAt4: true, looking in positions 5-11.

If BOTH positions are eligible, swap whichever has the LOWER bat rating first.
Only ONE swap is performed in total.

When a swap is executed:
  - The finisher is locked into that position.
  - All slots BELOW it are completely re-run through Phase 1-3 assignment
    with the remaining pool (to correctly re-order everyone else).


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
STEP 9: ALL-ROUNDER ORDERING FIX AT POSITION 8
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

If the player at position 8 (index 7) is a pure Batsman:

  1. If position 7 (index 6) is an All-Rounder -> swap them
     (AR moves to 7, batter drops to 8).
  2. After that swap, if position 6 (index 5) is also an All-Rounder ->
     swap positions 6 and 7 (the AR from step 1 moves further up).

Ensures All-Rounders sit above pure batters in the lower-middle order.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
STEP 10: BENCH ALL-ROUNDER UPGRADE FOR WEAK POSITION 8 (FIRST PASS)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Triggers if position 8 has a non-finisher Batsman or WK-Batsman
with currentBatting < 78.

Looks at all bench All-Rounders (not in the XII yet), sorted by
max(bat, bowl) descending. Tries to swap one in at position 8 —
must pass OS and bowler caps.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
STEP 11: HIGH-RATED BATTER PROMOTION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Triggers if position 8 has a non-finisher, non-bowler with currentBatting > 81.

Scans positions 3-7 for the player with the LOWEST batting rating. If that
player's bat rating is lower than the position-8 player's, swaps them —
moving the high bat-rated player up the order.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
STEP 12: BENCH BATTER UPGRADE FOR WEAK POSITION 8
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Triggers if position 8 still has a non-finisher Batsman or WK-Batsman
with currentBatting < 78.

Looks at bench Batsmen and WK-Batsmen (not onlyOpensOrBenched), sorted by
bat rating descending, tiebroken by battingAggression descending.

Upgrades position 8 if the bench player is strictly better (higher rating,
or same rating + higher aggression), and if caps allow.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
STEP 13: BENCH ALL-ROUNDER UPGRADE FOR WEAK POSITION 8 (SECOND PASS)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Final safety net. Triggers if position 8 still has a non-finisher Batsman
or WK-Batsman with currentBatting < 78.

Same as Step 10 but runs AFTER the batter upgrade attempt. Bench ARs must
have max(bat, bowl) > 74 and pass caps. The bowler cap is evaluated against
the PRE-SWAP lineup so the incoming AR doesn't tighten the cap against itself.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
OUTPUT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

The 11-player array returned is ordered positions 1-11, mapping back to the
players' REAL roles (the virtual role remapping from pre-processing is discarded).
